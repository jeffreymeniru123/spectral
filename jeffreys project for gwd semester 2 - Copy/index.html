ABOUT

Who we are

Open the door to Amys' Bookcafe and gallery and discover a locavore's delight. We take pride in serving only the best of coffee has to offer. Our menu selections are made with all natural ingredients provided by local suppliers and coffee roasters..

Why choose us?

While food is local, our vintage book collection is global and is located in the heart of our cafe. We carry books on architecture, food, poetry, art, fashion, philosophy, film, drama, cooking, and children's book, many in foreign languages including the largest collection of polish books. Look up at the walls and you'll see professionally curated exhibitions of fine photography, well lit and carefully represented, changing several times a year. Amy's Bookcafe and gallery is a culinary and cultural oasis in historic over the rhine..
